Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is famous for its traditional South Indian cuisine that is full of flavor, spice, and aroma. Most dishes are made with rice, lentils, and spices, and people often enjoy them with sambar, rasam, or chutneys.

Popular Foods in Chennai


  1. Idli & Dosa – Soft steamed rice cakes and crispy rice pancakes served with chutney and sambar.
  2. Vada – A crispy, fried snack made from lentils — perfect with tea or coffee.
  3. Sambar Rice – A hearty mix of rice, vegetables, and spicy lentil gravy.
  4. Pongal – A comforting dish made from rice and moong dal with ghee, pepper, and cashews.
  5. Biryani – Especially Chennai-style biryani, which has a rich, spicy flavor (like Dindigul or Ambur types).
  6. Filter Coffee – A strong, aromatic South Indian coffee served in a metal tumbler and davara.
  7. Chettinad Cuisine – Famous for its spicy, aromatic gravies and curries, especially chicken and mutton dishes.
  8. Street Foods – Sundal, bajji, murukku, and parotta with salna are favorites among locals.

    Sweets & Drinks

  1. Ukkarai: A traditional sweet dessert made from sweetened lentils, often served during festivals.
  2. Badam Milkr: A creamy, cold, and refreshing milk drink flavored with almonds and edible camphor.
  3. Filter Coffee: A distinct South Indian coffee served hot, often with frothed milk.
